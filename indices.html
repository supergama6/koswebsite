What are index CFDs?

Trade broader equity market movements

Index CFD trading lets traders take a view on the price movement of a basket of shares that represents a wider stock market or sector.

Index prices can respond to company earnings, economic data, interest rate expectations, geopolitical events and overall market sentiment.
