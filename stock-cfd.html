What are stock CFDs?

Trade share price moves without owning shares

Stock CFD trading lets traders follow price movement in individual listed companies without buying the underlying shares.

Stock prices can shift with earnings reports, company news, analyst expectations, sector momentum, interest rates and broad sentiment.
