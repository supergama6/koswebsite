What is Forex trading?

Trade changing global currency prices

Forex trading compares one currency with another. With Forex CFDs, traders can follow rising or falling currency pairs without holding the underlying currency.

Currency prices shift with interest rates, economic data, central bank policy, geopolitical events and market sentiment.
