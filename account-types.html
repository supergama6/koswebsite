Trading Account Guide

What does a trading account let you do?

A trading account gives you a secure route into global CFD markets. It lets you fund, access platforms, place trades and manage positions through one broker relationship.

✓

Market access with KOS Markets

A KOS Markets trading account connects you to forex, metals, commodities, indices and stock CFDs through MT5 and WebTrader.

Select Standard for spread-based pricing, or Raw for tighter spreads with separate commission costs.
