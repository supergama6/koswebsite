What are metal CFDs?

Trade price movements across key metal markets

Metal CFD trading lets traders take a view on price movements in markets such as gold, silver, platinum, palladium and copper without taking physical delivery.

Metal prices can respond to US dollar moves, bond yields, inflation expectations, supply constraints, central bank policy and global industrial activity.
