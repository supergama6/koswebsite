What are metal CFDs?

Trade price moves across core metal markets

Metal CFD trading lets traders follow price moves in markets such as gold, silver, platinum, palladium and copper without physical delivery.

Metal prices can shift with US dollar moves, bond yields, inflation expectations, supply conditions, central bank policy and industrial activity.
