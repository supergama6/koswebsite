How KOS Markets Handles Personal Information

Last updated: July 1, 2026

This privacy policy explains how KOS Markets Limited collects, uses and protects personal information when you visit this website, contact our team or submit information through our online forms.

Information We May Collect

We may collect details you provide directly, including your name, email address, phone number, country, account request details and messages sent through our website forms.

How We Use Information

We use information to respond to enquiries, provide account and platform support, process service requests, improve our website experience and communicate relevant updates.

Website Data

Our website may collect basic technical data such as browser type, device information, pages visited and general usage activity to help maintain performance and improve usability.

Sharing Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers who help us operate the website, deliver support or complete requested services.

Data Protection

We use reasonable administrative, technical and operational measures to help protect personal information from unauthorised access, loss, misuse or alteration.

Your Choices

You may contact us to request access, correction or deletion of personal information where available. You may also ask us to stop sending non-essential communications.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how your information is handled, please contact us at support@kosmarkets.com.
