Overnight Swaps

Understand overnight holding costs

Swap rates are rollover adjustments applied when leveraged CFD positions stay open after the daily market rollover time.

What is swap rate?

Overnight funding on open positions

A swap rate is the financing adjustment applied to an open CFD position held overnight. It is also called an overnight swap or rollover rate.

Depending on the product, trade direction and market rates, the swap can be charged to the account or credited to the account.

  • Swaps apply when a position stays open past rollover time.
  • Long and short positions can have different swap rates on the same instrument.
  • Swap rates may change as market rates, liquidity and broker conditions change.

How does a Forex swap work?

Rollover applies when a trade stays open overnight

At the end of the trading day, open positions can roll into the next trading day. That rollover can create a swap debit or credit on the account.

01

Open a position

Buy or sell a Forex pair or another CFD instrument through MT5 or WebTrader.

02

Hold past rollover

If the position remains open after platform rollover time, overnight financing is assessed.

03

Swap is applied

The account is adjusted with a swap charge or credit according to instrument and trade direction.

Wednesday rollover

Forex and some CFD products may apply a triple swap on Wednesday for weekend settlement. The exact day and amount can vary by instrument.

Formula guide

How swap rate is calculated

Position size x Swap rate x Holding days

The final adjustment can differ by symbol, trade direction, account currency and platform settings. Always check the live platform specification.

Instrument

Different Forex pairs, metals, commodities, indices and stock CFDs can have different swap structures.

Direction

Buy and sell positions may receive different overnight adjustments on the same market.

Lot size

Larger positions can create larger overnight financing adjustments.

Holding period

The longer a position stays open past rollover, the more swap adjustments may build up.
