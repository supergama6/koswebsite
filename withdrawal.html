Important Notes

Before you request a withdrawal

Withdrawal rules help keep client funds secure and payment records clear.

Same-name withdrawals

Withdrawals should be sent to a bank account, card or wallet held in the same name as your KOS Markets trading account.

No third-party destinations

Third-party withdrawal destinations may be rejected according to payment provider, bank and compliance requirements.

Processing may vary

Withdrawal times depend on the method, bank cut-off times, payment provider checks and account verification status.

Verification checks

KOS Markets may request additional documents before a withdrawal request is approved or processed.
