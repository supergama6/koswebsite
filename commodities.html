What are commodity CFDs?

Trade price moves across key global resources

Commodity CFD trading lets traders follow price moves in markets such as crude oil, natural gas, gold, silver and agricultural products without physical delivery.

Commodity prices can shift with supply, demand, inventory data, central bank policy, inflation expectations, weather conditions and geopolitical events.
