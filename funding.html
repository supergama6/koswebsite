Important Notes

Before you make a deposit

Funding rules help keep client accounts secure and payment records clear.

Same-name funding

Deposits should come from a bank account, card or wallet held in the same name as your KOS Markets trading account.

No third-party payments

Third-party payments may be rejected and returned according to payment provider, bank and compliance requirements.

Processing may vary

Deposit times depend on the funding method, bank cut-off times, payment provider checks and account verification status.

Verification checks

KOS Markets may request additional documents before a deposit is processed or credited to your trading account.
